Purpose
Scientific Grid Computing and Cloud Computing Resources for Community (SGCCRfC) is a pioneering website established in 2013 to commemorate and advance the innovative landscape of cloud computing and grid computing. As a non-profit organization, SGCCRfC serves as a central hub for scientists, engineers, and professionals across various fields on global scale, offering them the latest updates on state-of-the-art technologies and developments in the realm of computing resources. The platform is dedicated to facilitating community access to cutting-edge knowledge and resources, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing in these rapidly evolving domains. Through curated content, expert insights, and timely news, SGCCRfC empowers its users with comprehensive information on the advancements and practical applications of cloud and grid computing for their respective fields.

History
Scientific Grid Computing and Cloud Computing Resources for Community (SGCCRfC) was founded in 2013 with a vision to create a platform that connects the global scientific and technological community. The organization's inception was motivated by the rapid advancements in cloud computing and grid computing, as well as the growing need for collaboration and resource sharing among researchers, engineers, and professionals across various disciplines.


The founders of SGCCRfC recognized the potential of these emerging technologies to transform how research and innovation are conducted, making them more accessible and efficient. They aimed to establish a non-profit organization that would serve as a hub for the latest information, trends, and breakthroughs in cloud and grid computing.


From its inception, SGCCRfC prioritized fostering connections between academia and industry, creating opportunities for students and young professionals to engage with cutting-edge technologies. The organization also focused on promoting sustainability in computing and supporting initiatives that benefit the broader global community.


Over the years, SGCCRfC has grown into a respected authority in the fields of cloud and grid computing. It continues to organize events, workshops, and conferences that facilitate knowledge sharing and collaboration among experts. SGCCRfC's commitment to advancing the global community is evident in its dedication to empowering individuals with the resources and insights needed to thrive in the ever-evolving landscape of scientific and technological innovation.
